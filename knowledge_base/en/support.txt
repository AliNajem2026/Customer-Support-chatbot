ACCOUNT ACCESS & LOGIN
======================
If you cannot log in to your account, please follow these steps:
1. Visit the login page and click "Forgot Password".
2. Enter your registered email address to receive a reset link.
3. Check your spam/junk folder if you do not receive the email within 5 minutes.
4. If the account is locked after multiple failed attempts, wait 15 minutes and try again.
5. For persistent issues, contact our support team with your username and registered email.

To update your email address or username, go to Settings > Account > Edit Profile.
Two-factor authentication (2FA) can be enabled under Settings > Security.

BILLING & INVOICES
==================
You can view and download all invoices from your account dashboard under Billing > Invoices.
Invoices are generated on the first day of each billing cycle.
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal.
To update your payment method, go to Billing > Payment Methods > Add New.
If you were charged incorrectly, please submit a billing dispute within 30 days.
Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days back to the original payment method.
Annual plans offer a 20% discount compared to monthly plans.

TECHNICAL SUPPORT
=================
If the application is not loading, try the following:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
2. Try a different browser or device.
3. Disable browser extensions that may block scripts.
4. Check our status page at status.example.com for any ongoing incidents.

For mobile app issues, ensure the app is updated to the latest version.
To report a bug, go to Help > Report a Bug and provide as much detail as possible including screenshots.
API rate limits are set to 1000 requests per hour per account. Contact us to request an increase.
Data exports can be requested from Settings > Data > Export. Processing takes up to 24 hours.

GENERAL INFORMATION
===================
Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 6 PM (UTC).
Response times: Live chat — under 2 minutes; Email — within 24 hours.
To cancel your subscription, go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription.
After cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days before permanent deletion.
We are GDPR and CCPA compliant. View our full privacy policy at example.com/privacy.
